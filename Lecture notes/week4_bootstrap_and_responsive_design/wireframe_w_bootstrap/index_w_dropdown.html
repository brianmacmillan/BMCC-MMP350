My Project

Unproven Sexworker Perceptions



 The purpose of this research is to understand the extent to which people involved in what is perceived as voluntary sex work experience mental health problems and are exposed to psychological trauma related to their work. This study also aims to compare some common perceptions regarding the morality, background, and lifestyle of voluntary sex workers with the information provided by sex workers themselves.

Project Dialog

What I Wore When I Was Assaulted

Haley Bogandorff and Mikaela Cunningham

An assault can be physical or verbal. The clothing worn at the time of the assault can result in victim blaming. We created this website to help put an end to this practice, and to give assault survivors an anonymous place to be able to share their stories. The goal is to stop the stigma on all kinds of assaults such as those based on gender, sexual orientation (LGBTQ), religion, domestic relationships, and race.
